Wholesale Trade of Construction Machinery and Tools

We supply a wide range of construction machinery, equipment, and professional tools for companies operating in building, renovation, infrastructure development, and industrial sectors.

Heavy construction machinery

Our range includes excavators, loaders, bulldozers, compactors, cranes, and concrete mixers designed to handle demanding workloads, improve efficiency, and ensure safe, stable operation on construction sites.

Power Tools

A selection of durable and high-precision power tools essential for professional construction and renovation work. We supply drills, rotary hammers, grinders, circular saws, impact drivers, demolition tools, and other equipment built for reliability, accuracy, and continuous performance.

Hand Tools

Professional-grade hand tools for everyday use on construction sites and workshops. Our assortment includes hammers, trowels, screwdrivers, pliers, wrenches, measuring tapes, levels, and more - all designed to provide ergonomic handling, durability, and precise results.

Wholesale Trade of Solid, Liquid, and Gaseous Fuels

We supply a wide range of solid, liquid, and gaseous fuels for industrial enterprises, logistics companies, agricultural businesses, and heating facilities.

Solid Fuels

Reliable energy sources for heating systems, industrial boilers, metallurgical processes, and power generation. We supply high-calorific coal, eco-friendly pellets, wood briquettes, and coke — all tested for consistent combustion, low ash content, and optimal energy output.

Liquid Fuels

High-quality liquid fuels for transport, industrial machinery, generators, and heating systems. Our offer includes diesel, gasoline, heating oil, kerosene, and various technical oils. These fuels ensure efficient engine performance, lower emissions, and stable operation even under demanding conditions.

Gaseous Fuels

Clean and efficient energy sources used in heating, industrial processes, food production, and transportation. We provide natural gas, propane, butane, and LPG in bulk quantities. These fuels offer high energy efficiency, reduced environmental impact, and reliable performance for both commercial and industrial clients.

Trade in Industrial Equipment

We supply a wide range of industrial equipment designed to support manufacturing, production, warehousing, and various industrial processes. Our catalog includes modern, reliable, and energy-efficient solutions from trusted global manufacturers. Each product is selected to increase productivity, reduce operational costs, and ensure safe, uninterrupted operation.

Processing Machinery

High-performance equipment designed for efficient manufacturing and continuous industrial operations. We offer machinery for metalworking, woodworking, plastics processing, and fully automated or semi-automated assembly lines. Our solutions support cutting, forming, molding, welding, shaping, and assembling processes, enabling businesses to increase productivity, improve precision, and reduce production downtime.

Pumps and Compressors

Reliable pumping and compression systems essential for industrial production, fluid handling, and pressure control. Our assortment includes centrifugal and diaphragm pumps, industrial vacuum systems, piston and screw air compressors, and specialized pressure equipment. These solutions ensure stable operation, high efficiency, and safe handling of liquids, gases, and chemicals in various industrial environments.

Cooling Systems

Advanced systems designed to maintain safe working conditions and optimal process temperatures. We supply air handling units, industrial fans, cooling towers, heat exchangers, and ventilation components that support proper airflow, temperature regulation, and removal of harmful contaminants. These systems enhance workplace safety, improve energy efficiency, and prolong the lifespan of industrial machinery.

Power Supply

Essential electrical infrastructure for stable, uninterrupted industrial performance. Our product range includes transformers, switchgear, control panels, power distribution systems, voltage stabilizers, and protective devices. These solutions support the safe distribution of electricity, overload protection, automation control, and efficient energy management across production facilities.

Wholesale Trade of Electrical and Heating Equipment

We supply a wide range of electrical and heating equipment - including advanced inverters and backup power systems - for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Our products ensure stable power supply, efficient heating, and reliable operation of essential systems under all conditions.

Inverters

Our inverters and backup power systems provide clean, stable electricity during outages and voltage fluctuations. Generators
